Who is Virgil Abloh?

Virgil Abloh was a visionary designer and artist, known for founding the luxury streetwear brand Off-White and serving as the Artistic Director of Louis Vuitton's menswear. His work with Nike and Jordan Brand, including "The Ten" collection, redefined modern sneaker culture.
