Who is Tinker Hatfield?

Tinker Hatfield is a legendary designer at Nike, credited with some of the most iconic sneakers of all time. His work includes designs for the Air Jordan line and the development of the first cross-training shoe. He is celebrated as a pioneer in modern footwear design.
